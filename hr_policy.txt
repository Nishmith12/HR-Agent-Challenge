# COMPANY LEAVE POLICY
1. Casual Leave (CL): Employees are entitled to 12 days of CL per year. CL cannot be taken for more than 3 consecutive days.
2. Sick Leave (SL): 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate is required for more than 2 days of SL.
3. Maternity Leave: Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
4. Paternity Leave: Male employees are entitled to 5 days of paid paternity leave.

# REMOTE WORK POLICY
- Employees are allowed 2 days of Work From Home (WFH) per week.
- Core working hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
- All remote employees must be available on Slack/Teams during core hours.

# EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
- Travel: Economy class for domestic flights. Approval required for international.
- Food: Daily allowance of $50 when traveling for business.
- Internet: $30 monthly reimbursement for remote employees.
